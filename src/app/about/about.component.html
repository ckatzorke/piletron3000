About

Credits

Thimbleweed Park

The name PileTron 3000™ is inspired by Ron Gilbert's incredible awesome point & click adventure revival Thimbleweed Park (of course, it is no trademark by any means, and only part of the reference and hommage to Mr. Gilbert's amazing work).
It is available on almost any platform, so no excuse to not play it - if you look back with joy to game highlights like Monkey Island or Day of the Tentacle, go and play Thimbleweed Park.

Howlongtobeat

Howlongtobeat inspired me in the first place for creating PileTron 3000™. They also provide some similar functionality, but limited to Steam libraries and there are no suggestions what you could do in this time.
